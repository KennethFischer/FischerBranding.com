SKILLS

UX - Research

Is a careful and detailed study into a specific problem, concern, or issue using the scientific method. These can come from observing the world, prior research, professional literature, or from peers.

 

UI - Design

Is the process of making responsive user interfaces for desktop and mobile devices with a focus on the looks or style. Aimed to create designs that users will find easy to use and pleasurable to view.

 

Visual Design

Aims to shape the user experience through considering the effects of illustrations, photography, typography, layouts, and color on the usability of products and on their aesthetic appeal.

 

Art Direction

Brings clarity and definition to the visual process; it helps designs convey a specific message to a particular group of individuals. It also influences just about anything we interact with and see daily.
