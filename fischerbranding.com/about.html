Hi, my name is

Ken Fischer

I’m a UX / UI Branding Designer & Researcher.

I am an award-winning Branding Designer with decades of experience; developing creative that drives brands messages directly to their consumers. With the combination of creativity, project management, team leadership and technological skills, I have the strong ability to guide and direct multi disciplinary projects from design concept through completion.
I feel “design is so much more than making something attractive! It takes problem solving, strategic thinking and the ability to communicate anything to anyone when done right”!

I attended & graduated with a BFA from the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City, with a concentration in Consumer Packaging & Branding / minoring in Psychology. I then spent years working in both agencies & corporate, creating consumer branding, packaging & print collateral.


I have recently changed my focus to UX / UI Design to keep up with the changing times and for a new creative challenge! I do believe UX / UI design is the most exciting place for me to share my creativity & experience. “I love the fact that the research and testing depict & validate the design direction.

"This is why for the last 10 months during the evenings and weekends after working at my fulltime job as a Design Director I have been attending Bloc’s UI / UX Design Apprenticeship".
